About

By "classically minded", I mean a sensibility that promotes function over form. Visually, I rely on styles that I know work because I have seen them work. But I am most interested in the functionality and the content of the web page. I have always believed that those should do the proverbial "talking".

And as interested as I may be in developing web experiences that attract and inform users, I am even more intrigued by the (content). Informed by my liberal arts background, I am interested in just about any subject -- and shall prove one of the most interested laymen that you will ever work with.
